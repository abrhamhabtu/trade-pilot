## 📋 **Project Summary**

**TradePilot** is a comprehensive trading analytics platform that democratizes professional-grade trading tools. Built entirely on Bolt.new, it solves the critical problem of expensive trading journals ($40-50/month) that create barriers for aspiring traders who need performance tracking most.

### **The Problem**
- TradingView (most popular platform) has excellent charting but **zero journaling capabilities**
- Dedicated trading journals cost $40-50/month (TradeZilla, TradeSync)
- New traders can't afford proper analytics before they're even profitable
- 90% of traders fail partly due to lack of performance tracking

### **Our Solution**
- **Completely free** professional-grade trading analytics
- **AI-powered insights** based on actual trading patterns
- **TradingView integration** for seamless data import
- **Beautiful modern UI** that rivals expensive platforms
- **Comprehensive education** with detailed trading playbooks

### **📊 Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
- Real-time performance metrics (Net P&L, Profit Factor, Win Rate, Expectancy)

- Interactive charts: Cumulative P&L, Daily P&L, Radar performance visualization
- Time-based analysis to identify optimal trading hours
- Duration analysis to optimize holding periods

- **AI Trading Coach**: Personalized insights based on your actual data

### **📚 Trading Education**
